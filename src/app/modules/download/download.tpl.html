Downloading to Bloom

You are asking this web page to download {{book.title}} so that you can edit it locally on your computer.
In order to download files to Bloom, you must first install the right version of Bloom, which you can get here. (You will be asked to log in, but you can use the 'guest' login to do the download.)
When you are ready to proceed, click the Download button. You will initially see a rather scary message asking whether it is OK to have Bloom open a link. Just tell the browser to go ahead. After a short delay, the book should open in Bloom, ready for you to make your own book based on it. Most browsers have an option that will allow you to stop seeing the warning.
You can also tell Bloom to bypass this page and open books directly in future, by checking the box below. (Unfortunately, that won't suppress the warning from your browser...you will have to turn that off separately.)
